      Welcome to A107 Salon

      A107 Unisex Salon is strategically situated in the heart of the business hub
      of Lagos at #35, Adetokunbo Ademola Street, Victoria Island. We have a
      wide variety of seasoned proffessionals standing bye to attend to you in a classy,
      calm and condussive environment. try us today and you will surely experience
      hospitality at its finest.

      YOUR LOOKS
      RESTS ON THE SHOULDERS
      OF OUR GREAT TEAM

      Our services include:
      Hair Cut, Hair Dressing, Wigs and Braids, Makeup and Gele, Pedicure and Mannicure,
      dreadlocks and natural hair, Nails and Makeup academy.

      Hair Making

      We make Natural braids, Ghana weaves, weavon and wig fixing, natural and synthetic dread locks, fox locks, hair treatments and any kind of hair you have in mind to get made. We make you look stunning.

      Hair Cut

      Our proffesional barbers are equiped with the best clippers and blades, we pay detailed attention to every individuals hair reqirement as presented in texture and clients specifications.

      Makeover

      We understand that all clients have distinct skin types and requirements hence we have a wide range of first tier skin and makeup products formulated specially for you to look spectacular to your event of choice.

      Manicure

      We have the best products to make your hands smoothe, soft and fresh our team of dexterious proffesionals will give your hands the best treatment money can buy.

      VIP Area

      In trying to put our clients first in making them as comfortable as possible we provide a special area where you and your friends and family can have all the privacy you need.

      Gele Tying

      We know after working hard there is need to unwind, if its a wedding, an introduction a naming ceremony or any event at that we can give you that unforgetable head accesory (Gele) with makeup to go with it.

      Nail Fixing

      We stock the best Acrylics, Gels and Silk wraps and even let you bring your nail products, give us your fingers and lets make them them the envy of others.

      Wig Making

      We build all kinds of wigs from scratch to fit clients specifications we also provide wig washing, repairs and fixing services.
